## Context

This was the first meeting between Eco and B2C2. The meeting was initiated to explore whether B2C2 could provide USDG liquidity access to Eco via their Penny platform. The conversation was introductory — no prior formal engagement. The meeting was initially misclassified as a triage session by the Granola Watcher (both EPD leads were present), but correctly identified by the Triage Listener as a partner/BD meeting and escalated to the Partner Intelligence pipeline.

---

## Decisions

- **[Explore Penny platform]** The Eco team agreed to evaluate B2C2's Penny platform as a potential USDG liquidity source. Both credit line and pre-funded trade models were discussed as viable mechanics to explore further.
- **[White-label option open]** API integration and white-labeling of the Penny platform were floated as possibilities, to be assessed once API specs are available.
- **[NDA first]** Both parties agreed that an NDA exchange is the required first step before sharing commercial or technical details.
